## 1. General Information

The Naira Index (NXY) is a blockchain-native benchmark designed to provide a transparent and reproducible reference indicator representing the relationship between the Nigerian Naira and the global U.S. Dollar benchmark environment represented by the US Dollar Index (DXY).

The protocol publishes benchmark observations derived from publicly observable macroeconomic inputs and validated through smart contract infrastructure.

The information contained within this repository, including all documentation, whitepapers, methodology specifications, and analytical materials, is provided for informational and research purposes only.

- execute currency trades  
- operate a foreign exchange market

- provide liquidity or price discovery services

Instead, the protocol records benchmark observations representing the relationship between the Nigerian Naira and the global dollar benchmark environment.
